Key Findings

  • Kakimasu excels in Hiragana/Katakana learning and writing practice, outperforming most competitors
  • Only Kanji Study matches Kakimasu's writing capabilities, but lacks Kakimasu's comprehensive approach
  • Duolingo leads in gamification but falls short in writing practice and kanji learning
  • LingoDeer offers the best grammar instruction but doesn't prioritize writing skills
  • Kakimasu offers the most balanced approach for beginners focused on developing proper writing skills
